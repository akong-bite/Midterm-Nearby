The Disappearance of Physical "Nearby":
From Space to Network

"Nearby" refers to a network of relationships based on geography, familiarity, and everyday interactions. It is an interdependent social support system that relies on face-to-face interactions and trust.

Physical "nearby" is not merely a measure of geographic distance but represents social connections and interactions formed through adjacent spaces and familiar communities (e.g., greetings at the market, neighborly exchanges).
